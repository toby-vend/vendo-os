## Decision Filters

### The Priority Filter

When two things compete for attention, this is the order:

1. Client retention and service quality
2. Revenue growth and new business
3. Profit margin improvement (via AI and automation)
4. Hiring key roles
5. Systems and infrastructure

### The Leverage Filter

Before recommending an action, the AI OS should ask:

- Does this improve profit margin without sacrificing quality?
- Can this be automated so staff only need to QA the output?
- Does this scale — will it still work at 80 clients the same as 40?

### The Quality Filter

- Every client-facing output must be QA'd by a human before delivery
- Speed matters, but not at the expense of client results
- Data-backed decisions only — no guesswork
